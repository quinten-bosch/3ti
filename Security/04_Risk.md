# Risk
> 100% secure does not exist
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

## Risk assessment

```
Risk 
= 
Probability x Impact
=
Likelihood x Damage
```

Decreasing risk &rarr; decreasing probability AND/OR decresing impact.

Risk:
- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Avoid
- Accept

### Generic Risk Assessment Methodology
1. Identify assets
2. Identify threats
3. Evaluate risks
4. Treat risks
    - Mitigate
    - Avoid
    - Transfer
    - Accept
5. List residual risk
    - Risk registry
